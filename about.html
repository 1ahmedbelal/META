Who We Are

The Mississippi Engineering Teachers Association (META) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing engineering education across Mississippi. We represent middle school, high school, and post-secondary educators committed to preparing the next generation of problem-solvers and innovators.

Our Focus

  • Professional development and training
  • Annual conferences and student competitions
  • Advocacy for engineering in STEM curricula
  • Real-world practice in classrooms

Milestones

  1. 2010

    META founded by a coalition of Mississippi engineering educators.

  2. 2015

    Launched statewide student design challenge.

  3. 2020

    Shifted to hybrid PD with virtual workshops across districts.

  4. 2025

    Resource library expanded with new curricula and grants.
