About META

Building a stronger engineering education community across Mississippi.

Who We Are

The Mississippi Engineering Teachers Association (META) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing engineering education in the state of Mississippi. We represent middle school, high school, and post-secondary educators who are passionate about preparing the next generation of problem-solvers and innovators.

What We Do

  • Provide professional development and training for engineering educators
  • Host annual conferences and student competitions
  • Advocate for engineering in STEM curricula at all educational levels
  • Support the integration of real-world engineering practices in classrooms

Our Vision

To ensure every Mississippi student has access to high-quality engineering education, empowering them to lead in a technology-driven world.
